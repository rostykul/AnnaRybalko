Get to know me

My name is Anna Rybalko and I am a Dancer and a certified Ballet Instructor who is currently studying to become a chiropractor at the Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College. I have been teaching ballet technique, conditioning for dancers and pointe for over 10 years now. I have also had the pleasure to perform with an entertainment company for nearly 8 years. The experience I have as a dancer and dance teacher have nudged me to begin online one on one dance coaching. My goal is to help dancers understand and embody dance technique through personalized online coaching sessions that focus on a specific aspect of training to reach the dancers training goals. My approach focuses on the dancer's anatomy and personalized conditioning of the body for optimal performance in dance.

About dance coaching

Dance Coaching was designed to be a one on one online, tailored dance technique improvement and education sessions. In our sessions we will dissect technique and learn to embody the necessary coordinations and movement patterns. We will also focus on injury prevention and prehab, to keep you healthy and dancing for a long time. The sessions will focus to suit your dance goal and your personal anatomy. Book a session if you are interested in: improving balance and turns, foot work, pre-pointe preparations, ballet arms, turn out, flexibility and jumps OR any other aspects of technique which you want to improve.
